Youth of India is a Youth led movement, evolved from the successful and innovative campaigns hosted by Youth of Siliguri, since 2015. Youth of India is weaved out of Youth of Siliguri, which has been appreciated for its innovative campaigns as it was projected in the side event at United Nations General Assembly in 2015.

Further, we are one of the 174 organizations across the globe to be granted special accreditation by the United Nations for 2020 Ocean Conference and one of the 54 CSOs to be granted special accreditation for the 31st special session of the United Nations General Assembly. Currently, we have also been accredited for the historic Stockholm+50 and have been inducted as a member of C20 (Civil Engagement Group of G20) hosted by the Government of Indonesia.

ABOUT YOUTH OF INDIA MUN

Youth of India Model United Nations (YOI MUN), is organized by Youth of Indian Foundation, which has been given special accreditation by the United Nations.

With the vision of building an inclusive movement for engaging, empowering and evolving youth, YOI brings to the young leaders and the future diplomats an opportunity to be a part of the biggest Model UN conference from 21st April, 2023 to 23rd April, 2023.

Youth of India MUN 2023 is being hosted in collaboration with United Nations Information Center for India & Bhutan, C20 (official engagement group of G20) and others. Youth of India MUN2023 is also in talks with various other national & international agencies, organisations, governments and companies. With such vast outreach and support, YOIMUN looks forward to targeting, influencing and hosting students and future diplomats from all 29 States, 7 Union Territories of India and 193 countries of the world.

YOIMUN strives to provide the students and the learners of the society with a chance to learn and deliberate upon the geopolitical developments in the world. We also look forward to making contributions in achieving the 2030 agenda of the United Nations by making efforts to host around students, future leaders and debaters from around the globe.
